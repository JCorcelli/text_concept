On the offbeat part of Bridgemond there is a ramshackle grove where a cabin lay. The cabin belongs to a family of loose Christians. They became tolerant of moral ambiguity after some time and their daughter Priscilla who was old enough, was allowed to take care of the cabin while they weren't in need of it.


In a nether realm, magicians and wizards battle for conquest. Hak was nothing more than a trickster in his craft, a neophyte among great demonic warlords.

Indeed, he was not a man at birth, mere moments before his arrival into our world he was more like a shade with few physical desires.
