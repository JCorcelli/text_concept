LITTLE MAID
In a mansion the cute little plush bunny is zooming around wiping up a dusty room that's been unused for weeks.  Later the maids would enter and find an immaculate dining room, "How did you get all this work done without me knowing." One would accuse the other.  They'd be left in a confused tiffy at that.  The little plush bunny does a binky and after stuffing the dust into a bin scoots out the room.
