The anthromorphs are various animals who have taken on human shape. The way they behave is closer to human than beast.

Other plants and objects have also taken on shapes of humanoids, among other things.

Although by appearance you may assume the animals are more animal than human, that is not usually the case. The thing that the wildlife desires the most is attention, and someone to get to know.  Even the wild intelligent trees like to get to know you.
