Four is a sprawling cavernous wild place. The ever-changing scenery makes landmarks difficult to find.

There are man made landmarks. The district four citizens have been careful enough to maintain several stations, laboratories, safety houses; Paths are often cleared on several main ways.

The places that never change in district four are its central core, the ground and sky above the North, and small portions of the land which have been tunneled through relentlessly and reinforced from district three.
