The architecture in Three is very nature oriented. The wooden buildings that are cut out of the landscape have engraved leaves and floral patterns.

The most intricate detail is in the landscaping, the plants, because they are vital to the infrastructure and survival of the original settlement. Not only that, they add visual appeal.

The landscape floor is both man made and autonomously grown from the giant tree. The waterways are different, sap laden, milky, salty, and fresh; each create their own unique ecosystems around their property.

A little of both worlds exists here in district three, despite how well the angels attempt to preserve the history an occasional innovation has made its way in with tourists.

The animals here show their affection openly to each other and humanoids, and they both have familial designs like humans. The humanoids equally embrace the animal nature of open affection.
