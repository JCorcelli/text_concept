MOB BLOCKS / TOYS

		
	any block
		hold e - sniff, eat
		idle - may eat
		init signal - observe (get menu)
		
		
	mob block (A)
		move = no effect

		e - pick up or push
		init B > A - use B on A
		init A > bun - swap position
		init bun > A - stand on
		
		Add Force
		attack + direction - force in direction
		
	held block
		held
		move = slowed
		
		e - swap with
		F - drop
		F + mouse + direction - throw
		init A > B - store in, place on, combine
		init B > Bun - swap A with B
		init signal - context (get menu)
		
		q - store in inventory (eat)
