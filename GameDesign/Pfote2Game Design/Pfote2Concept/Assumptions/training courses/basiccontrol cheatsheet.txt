Basic controls

arrow (wasd) - move
Mouse
	move, run, look
shift to get ready to jump, click
hold shift, for fun useful effect

space/escape to cancel any selections or multiple step actions

space to zone
zone has a big impact, changing the screen into a collage of selectable objects. select bunny here and click to move.

ctrl to alt zone
c for camera
q / i for inventory
number keys for shortcuts
e / enter to use / consume
f - defend / skill use
hold f + click - throw

Escape will pause if you're safe, otherwise it's only useful to quit
